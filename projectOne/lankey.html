Welcome

Welcome to LAN-KEY Gaming, where you can unlock the power of fun. We have several events each month where you can meet with other CIT Students who enjoy playing games. We have a wide variety of gaming opportunities to meet any interest. Our most popular games are Classic Arcade, Roleplaying, and Dance, but we have many others.

There are many benefits to being a member of our Club. Members can receive a discount on some game products, as well as early notice on new games. We welcome those who are new to gaming as well as those with lots are experience. The Club has several short game sessions, and also longer tournaments where you can win big prizes from our sponsors, inaddition to bragging rights.
